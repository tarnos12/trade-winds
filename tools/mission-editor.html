Help✕
Drag a mission card to move it into another grid slot.
Connect prerequisites: click one of the 8 handle circles on a source card to arm it, then click any handle (or the body) of another card — this makes the source a prerequisite of the target (arrow points source → target). Click empty space or press Esc to cancel.
Delete a card or a prerequisite line: click it, then press Delete.
Edit a mission's name, icon, the retroactive flag and its objectives in the Inspector on the right.
Scroll to zoom, drag empty space to pan.
