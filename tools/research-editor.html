Research Tree Editor — Trade Winds

Help✕
Drag a card body (its centre) to move it into another slot.
Connect two cards: click one of the 8 connection circles (4 corners + 4 edge mid-points) to arm it as the source, then click a circle on another card to link them (source → target). The line attaches to the exact two circles you click. Click empty space or press Esc to cancel.
Click a connector line or a card, then press Delete to remove it.
Resize a band by dragging the small gold-outlined square in the bottom-right corner of its lane (right → columns, down → rows).
Upgrade pips sit above a building's card — select one to edit its ladder level.
Kingdom research lives in the left column and is always prerequisite-free.
Select a prerequisite line to fine-tune which side or corner of each card it attaches to (4 edges + 4 corners); multiple lines on the same edge fan out along it.
Scroll to zoom, drag empty space to pan.
